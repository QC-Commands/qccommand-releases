Questions

Frequently asked

More detailed troubleshooting lives in the full FAQ.

Which headphones are supported?

Bose QC Ultra and QC Ultra 2, connected over Bluetooth. Other Bose models using the same protocol may work but aren't officially tested.

Do I need the Bose Music app installed?

No — but keep it closed while using QC Command. It can hold the Bluetooth connection and block mode switching.

"QCCommand is damaged and can't be opened"?

Not real damage — a Gatekeeper quirk with non-notarized apps. Run xattr -cr /Applications/QCCommand.app once in Terminal and it's gone for good.

How does the lifetime license work?

Pay once, use it forever. It activates on one device at a time — deactivate from the About tab to move it to a different Mac.

What happens when my trial ends?

The app locks behind a screen asking for a license key. Your settings stay intact and unlock instantly the moment you activate.

Is there a subscription?

No. €10.99 once, and the license is yours — no renewals, no recurring charges, ever.
